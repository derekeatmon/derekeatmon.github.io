The start of a new adventure

By Derek Eatmon

My name is Derek Eatmon. I was in the United States Army form 2006-2013. I was given retirement and medical discharged after getting injured in Afghanistan and a few surgeries later. I then started to work for the Department of Defense as a Systems Architect for three years on contract then that contract ended. I wanted to work for myself so I decided to get licensed in Life and Health insurance. While all of this is going on, I was in school and finally graduated in with a bachelors degree in Animation Technology. In the military, I worked as a signal support systems tech and worked on things from radios, crypto, satellite, and networking. I have limited knowlege in coding and want to know a lot more so I can be a success in that field.
